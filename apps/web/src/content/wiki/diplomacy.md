# Diplomacy

> Alliances, war, and trust in Season 4.

---

## Relations

| Status | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| **Neutral** | Default — no obligations |
| **Alliance Pending** | Proposal sent, awaiting response |
| **Allied** | Full alliance with trust tiers |
| **War Pending** | War declared, 24-hour countdown |
| **War** | Active hostilities — auto-war triggers |
| **Peace Pending** | Peace proposed, awaiting response |

## Alliances

Allies share:
- **Trust tiers** (0→3) — higher tiers give trade bonuses
- **Escrow pools** — shared gold/food for emergencies
- **Battlefield coordination** — can join each other's battles

| Trust Tier | Cargo Bonus | Escrow Pool |
|------------|-------------|-------------|
| 1 | +10% | 500g / 500f |
| 2 | +20% | 1,500g / 1,500f |
| 3 | +35% | 3,000g / 3,000f |

**Betrayal** instantly starts war. Betrayed ally keeps escrow pool.

## War

1. **Declare war** → 24-hour warning (unless casus belli)
2. **Casus belli**: detected raid → instant war
3. **Auto-war**: fronts auto-create, battalions auto-attack
4. **Peace**: either side can propose → acceptance resets to Neutral

## Managing Relations

All diplomacy is in **Castle → Diplomacy** tab. From there you can:
- Propose/accept alliances
- Declare war
- Propose peace
- Upgrade alliance trust
- View active relations

---

## Tips

- **Alliances reduce front exposure** — fewer enemies means more focused attacks
- **Trust tiers pay off** — the trade bonus compounds with Space Murine skills
- **Betrayal is permanent** — marked on your record
- **Casus belli** gives 24 hours to strike without warning
