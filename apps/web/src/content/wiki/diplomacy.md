# Diplomacy

> Alliances, war, peace, and politics in Season 4.

---

## Relation States

| State | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| NEUTRAL | Default. Can trade, propose alliance, declare war. |
| ALLIANCE_PENDING | Alliance proposed, waiting for acceptance. |
| ALLIED | Active alliance. Both sides have escrow locked. |
| WAR_PENDING | War declared, 24h countdown before combat starts. |
| WAR | Active war. Auto-war dispatches ATTACK battalions. |
| PEACE_PENDING | Peace proposed, waiting for acceptance. |
| ENEMY | Casus belli active. Immediate war available. |

## Alliances

**Proposing:** You can include optional terms — gold, food, army, or a tile deed. Terms can be one-sided (you pay them, or they pay you).

**Accepting:** Both sides deposit escrow (2,000 gold + 2,000 food at tier 1). The offered resources/tile are transferred immediately.

**Trust tiers:** Higher tiers increase escrow and trade delivery bonuses (+10%/+15%/+25%).

**Betrayal:** Betrayer's escrow is forfeited to the victim. War starts immediately (no 24h delay).

## War

**Declaration:** From NEUTRAL or ENEMY. 24-hour warning period before combat starts.

**Casus belli:** Detected covert raids grant immediate war declaration rights for 24h.

**Auto-war:** Once at war, ATTACK-mode battalions automatically attack connected enemy tiles. Set aggression on war fronts (CAUTIOUS/BALANCED/AGGRESSIVE).

## Peace

**Proposing:** Can include reparations — gold, food, army, or a tile deed from the proposer to the other side.

**Accepting:** Reparations are transferred. All active campaigns are canceled. A **24-hour unbreakable peace** begins — neither side can declare war or betray during this period.

## Diplomacy Page

Access from Castle → Diplomacy tab or the Politics link. Shows all other fortresses with available actions, trade offers, convoy status, and raid controls.
