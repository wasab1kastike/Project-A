# Getting Started

> How to join and survive your first hour in Season 4.

---

## 1. Sign In

Sign in with your Google account. No password needed.

## 2. Join a Season

When a cycle is open (Registration, Testing, or Active):
1. Click "Join this season" in the side panel
2. Choose your **commander name** (public nickname)
3. Name your **fortress**
4. Pick your **race** — see [Races](races) for details
5. Click "Join"

Your race is **locked for the season**. Choose carefully.

## 3. First Moves

| Step | What To Do | Where |
|------|-----------|-------|
| Assign workers | Set miners, farmers, recruiters | Castle → Economy |
| Commission battalion | Create a battalion, set mode to GUARD/ATTACK | Castle → Battalions |
| Set battalion stance | FORTIFY (defense), PATROL (scout), TRAINING (XP) | Castle → Battalion dropdown |
| Claim land | Assign pressure workers to expand | Castle → Economy |
| Open trade | Send gold, food, army, or tile deeds | Politics → Trade |
| Make allies | Propose alliance (can include resource terms) | Politics → Diplomacy |
| Prepare war | Declare war, auto-war dispatches ATTACK battalions | Politics → Diplomacy |

## 4. Battalion Modes & Stances

Your army is organized into **battalions**. Each battalion has:

**Mode** — determines automated behavior:
- 🛡 **GUARD**: Defends your tiles, auto-garrisons, patrols adjacent territory
- ⚔ **ATTACK**: Auto-dispatches to war fronts, attacks enemy tiles
- 📦 **RESERVE**: Passively heals 2% per tick, doesn't fight
- 🤝 **ALLIANCE**: Auto-reinforces allied battlefields

**Stance** — affects combat bonuses:
- 🏰 **FORTIFY**: +30% defense, -50% casualties taken (1h lock)
- 🚶 **PATROL**: +25% raid detection, requires garrisoned tile
- 🎯 **TRAINING**: +1 XP/tick for lowest-tier battalion
- 🗡 **AMBUSH**: +40% first-round damage, enemies can't retreat (1h lock)
- 💤 **REST**: +5 morale/tick, +2% heal/tick, auto-retreats if attacked
- 🏃 **MOBILE**: Flat bonuses, freely switchable

## 5. The Map

Your fortress appears on the hex map. You can:
- **Click tiles** to see ownership, pressure heatmap, and bonuses
- **Watch battalions** patrol as colored markers with stance badges
- **See roads** build as units and convoys travel (dirt → stone → highway)
- **Monitor battlefields** with army counts, progress bars, and intensity glow
- **Follow trade routes** — golden lines between established trading partners
- **Track reinforcements** — marching unit sprites when battalions gain troops

## 6. Economy Basics

| Resource | How To Get | Uses |
|----------|-----------|------|
| Gold | Miners (3/tick each) + tile bonuses | Upgrades, promotions, trade |
| Food | Farmers (2/tick each) + tile bonuses | Army upkeep |
| Army | Recruiters (3/tick each) + tile bonuses | Battalions, combat |
| Points | Tile income (1-3/tick), combat, trade, claiming tiles | Leaderboard — win condition |

Set a **max army cap** in War Room to control growth. **Reserves** show unassigned army.

**New in scoring overhaul:** Tiles generate points based on biome (mountains/coast=3, forest/hills=2, others=1). Roads add +1 pt/tick per connected tile. Winning combat grants bonus glory points beyond loot.

## 7. Expansion

Claim tiles by assigning **pressure workers**. Each worker generates 1 pressure per tick on a neighboring tile. At 600 pressure, the tile flips to you — and you get 5 bonus points.

The **pressure heatmap** shows territory strength: owned tiles shift from biome color toward your race color as pressure rises. Low-pressure tiles look vulnerable.

Owned tiles **decay** over time (-2/tick). Assign pressure workers to maintain them (1 worker ≈ 2 tiles). Guards slow decay by 50%.

## 8. Combat

War is automatic in Season 4:
1. Declare war via Diplomacy tab (24h warning unless casus belli)
2. Set battalion mode to ATTACK and stance as desired
3. Battalions auto-dispatch to attack connected enemy tiles
4. Units march across the map (visible with path animations)
5. Battlefields form on arrival — see crossed swords with army counts
6. Combat resolves automatically each tick

Set **guard %** to protect your territory. Guards auto-join battlefields on defense.

**Aggression** on war fronts controls commit rate: Cautious 30%, Balanced 60%, Aggressive 100%.

## 9. Diplomacy & Trade

**Alliances:** Propose with optional resource/tile terms. Both sides lock escrow against betrayal. Trust tiers (1-3) increase trade bonuses.

**Peace treaties:** Can include reparations (gold, food, army, tiles). Accepted peace has a **24-hour unbreakable period** — neither side can declare war.

**Trade:** Send resources via convoys. Established routes (5+ deliveries) get 1.25× point bonus. Convoys build roads as they travel. Tile deeds let you transfer territory to allies.
