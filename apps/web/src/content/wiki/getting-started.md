# Getting Started

> How to join and survive your first hour in Season 4.

---

## 1. Sign In

Sign in with your Google account. No password needed.

## 2. Join a Season

When a cycle is open (Registration, Testing, or Active):
1. Click "Join this season" in the side panel
2. Choose your **commander name** (public nickname)
3. Name your **fortress**
4. Pick your **race** — see [Races](races) for details
5. Click "Join"

Your race is **locked for the season**. Choose carefully.

## 3. First Moves

| Step | What To Do | Where |
|------|-----------|-------|
| Assign workers | Set miners, farmers, recruiters | Castle → Economy |
| Check army | View battalions, set guard % | Castle → War Room |
| Claim land | Assign pressure workers to expand | Castle → Economy |
| Make allies | Propose alliances | Castle → Diplomacy |
| Prepare war | Declare war, battalions auto-attack | Castle → Diplomacy |

## 4. The Map

Your fortress appears on the hex map. You can:
- **Click tiles** to see ownership, pressure, and bonuses
- **Watch battalions** march as colored markers
- **See roads** build as units travel
- **Monitor battlefields** as they resolve

## 5. Economy Basics

| Resource | How To Get | Uses |
|----------|-----------|------|
| Gold | Miners (3/tick each) | Upgrades, promotions, trade |
| Food | Farmers (2/tick each) | Army upkeep |
| Army | Recruiters (3/tick each) | Battalions, combat |
| Points | Everything above | Leaderboard — win condition |

Set a **max army cap** in War Room to control growth. **Reserves** show unassigned army.

## 6. Expansion

Claim tiles by assigning **pressure workers**. Each worker generates 1 pressure per tick on a neighboring tile. At 600 pressure, the tile flips to you.

Owned tiles **decay** over time (-2/tick). Assign pressure workers to maintain them (1 worker ≈ 2 tiles). Guards slow decay by 50%.

## 7. Combat

War is automatic in Season 4:
1. Declare war via Diplomacy tab
2. Battalions auto-dispatch to attack
3. Units march across the map (visible)
4. Battlefields form on arrival
5. Combat resolves automatically

Set **guard %** to protect your territory. Guards auto-join battlefields on defense.
