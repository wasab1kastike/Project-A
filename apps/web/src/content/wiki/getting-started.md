# Getting Started

> How to join a season and set up your fortress.

---

## Joining a Season

1. **Sign in** with your Google account
2. During the **Registration** phase, click "Join Season"
3. Pick a **fortress name** (unique per season)
4. Register a **commander name** (unique per season)
5. Choose your **race** — see [Races](races) for details
6. Choose your **doctrine** (Season 4 only) — pick the playstyle that fits you

## Picking Your Race

| Race | Difficulty | Style |
|------|-----------|-------|
| **Dwarfs** | Medium | Defensive, grudge-driven, mining expeditions |
| **Orks** | Easy | Aggressive, snowball through combat, Waaagh! |
| **Space Murines** | Hard | Trade logistics, rapid response, convoy networks |
| **Unstable Unicorns** | Chaos | Unpredictable, reality-bending, high variance |

Your race and doctrine are **locked for the season** after the Testing phase starts. Choose carefully!

## Your First Moves

1. **Assign workers** — miners, farmers, recruiters. Balance gold, food, and army.
2. **Claim nearby tiles** — use pressure workers to expand your territory.
3. **Build upgrades** — improve your fortress economy and military.
4. **Scout the map** — see who's nearby. Make friends or prepare defenses.

## Phases

| Phase | What To Do |
|-------|-----------|
| **Registration** | Join, pick name + race |
| **Testing** | Verify mechanics, plan strategy |
| **Active** | Play — economy, combat, diplomacy |
| **Resolution** | Season ends, winner crowned |

Mid-season joining locks partway through Active phase. Join early!

## The Map

Your fortress starts at a random location. The world is a hex grid — each tile can be claimed, pressured, or fought over. See [Economy](economy) for tile mechanics and [Combat](combat) for how to take tiles from enemies.
