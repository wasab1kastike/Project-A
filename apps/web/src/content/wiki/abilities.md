# Race Abilities

> Skill trees and unique mechanics in Season 4.

---

## Skill Trees

Each race has 3 paths with 8 nodes each. Earn points from castle level (+1 per level) and owned tiles (+1 per 3 tiles). Max 12 points.

Nodes unlock linearly per path — you must purchase previous nodes first.

## Dwarf Skills

**Race trait**: +10% defense. Gold per 10 miners: +1.

## Field Promotion

Pay gold to instantly promote a battalion to the next tier:

| From → To | Base Cost |
|-----------|----------|
| Recruit → Regular | 2,000g |
| Regular → Veteran | 8,000g |
| Veteran → Elite | 25,000g |

Plus 8g per unit in the battalion.

## Upkeep (Tiered)

| Tier | Food/100 units | Gold/Battalion |

| Regular | 2 | 1 |
| Veteran | 3 | 2 |
